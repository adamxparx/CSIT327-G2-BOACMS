Documentary Requirements

Please prepare the following documents for your appointment

Please prepare the original and one (1) photocopy of the following documents to bring on your appointment day.

1. Barangay Clearance

  • Valid Government-Issued ID (Passport, Driver's License, UMID, Voter's ID)
  • Proof of Residency (Utility bill, rental contract, or ID with your address)
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)

2. Certificate of Indigency

  • Valid Government-Issued ID
  • Proof of Residency
  • Proof of Income (or Affidavit of No Income)

3. Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)

  • Valid Government-Issued ID
  • Proof of Income (latest payslip, ITR, or certification of salary)

4. Solo Parent Certificate

  • Valid Government-Issued ID
  • Birth Certificate/s of your child/children
  • Proof of Solo Parent Status:
    • For Widows/Widowers: Death Certificate of the spouse
    • For Unmarried Parents: CENOMAR and Affidavit of Solo Parent
    • For Separated/Annulled: Judicial decree of separation/annulment
    • For Abandonment: Police report or Affidavit of Abandonment

List of Documentary Requirements

Please prepare the original and one (1) photocopy of the following documents to bring on your appointment day.

1. Barangay Clearance

  • Valid Government-Issued ID: (e.g., Passport, Driver's License, UMID, Voter's ID)
  • Proof of Residency: (e.g., Utility bill, rental contract, or ID with your address)
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula): (Some barangays may require this)

2. Certificate of Indigency

  • Valid Government-Issued ID: (e.g., Passport, Driver's License, UMID, Voter's ID)
  • Proof of Residency: (e.g., Utility bill, rental contract)
  • Proof of Income (or lack thereof): (e.g., latest payslip showing low income, or an Affidavit of No Income)

3. Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)

  • Valid Government-Issued ID: (e.g., Passport, Driver's License, UMID, Voter's ID)
  • Proof of Income: (e.g., latest payslip, ITR, or certification of salary)

4. Solo Parent Certificate (Barangay Certification)

  • Valid Government-Issued ID: (e.g., Passport, Driver's License, UMID, Voter's ID)
  • Birth Certificate/s of your child/children.
  • Proof of Solo Parent Status (Bring the document that applies to you):
    • For Widows/Widowers: Death Certificate of the spouse.
    • For Unmarried Parents: Certificate of No Marriage (CENOMAR) and an Affidavit of Solo Parent.
    • For Separated/Annulled: Judicial decree of legal separation or annulment.
    • For Abandonment: Police report or Affidavit of Abandonment.
